About Muwahid Travels

Sincerity in service, since day one.

Based in Ugoki, Sialkot, we specialise in Umrah packages and air ticket bookings for the Gulf region — guiding you through visas, flights, and accommodation with care and honesty at every step.

  • Customised Umrah packages for individuals, families & groups
  • Air ticket bookings for UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman & Kuwait
  • Visa facilitation and documentation support
  • Hand-picked hotels near Masjid al-Haram & Masjid an-Nabawi
  • Airport meet & assist, and group transport coordination
